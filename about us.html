About Us

TechQuest is a platform where Malawian youth interact, share and discuss different technological ideas. These ideas will help in the development and advancement of technology and science in Malaw. We are passionate about fostering innovation and technology by sharing different ideas and plans of how one wants to carry out his/her innovation or anything skill that will contribute to the advancement of our societies and Malawi as a country. Thank you!
