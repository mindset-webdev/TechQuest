What is Tech Quest?

+

Tech Quest is where youth in Malawi and share different technological ideas for the betterment of Malawi. This is even in alignment with the Malawi 2063 vision.

How many people are in Tech Quest?

+

Currently, TQ consists of 7 individuals, who are all under the age of 18.

Why is it necessary for me to take part in TQ?

+

Taking part in Tech Quest will help other people acknowledge your ideas and help in adjusting the ideas where it will be necessary. This will also help other people to have a mindset of developing the country and not completely relying on other sources for help.
